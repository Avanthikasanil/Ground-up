Ground up
Psychedelic Institute.

Ground up Psychedelic Institute supports Mental Health practitioners
to reorient their Psychotherapeutic approach to support their clients
Bottom up (felt lead) processing, which is activated whilst participating
in Psychedelic Medicine experiences.

Psychdeic Medicine experiences are emergent, intra-relational, felt
and here-and-now encounters. We help therapists link emergent,
intra-relational, felt lead, and here and now approaches to
Psychedelic Assisted Psychotherapy.

Emergent

Emergent- arising within the broader whole of existence ; An interplay from which ones experience organically emerges . This is quite opposite to a reduction into

Intra-relational

Intra-relational- a process of relating dynamically and internally with alive aspects of ourselves.

Felt

Felt – Visceral and somatic information becomes infinitely more accessible and interpretable. The experience is every aspect is sensation lead.

Here and now

Here and now - present. A deeper capacity to be in and with what is happening now, primarily; again via sensation lead information

About Robbie

Robbie has been in clinical private practice for over 18 years and has
explored his own plant medicine path for over 14 years, having
worked therapeutically and extensively with 16 Psychedelic and Plant
medicines himself; He has also worked with over 300 clients clinically
to prepare for and integrate their own Psychedelic Medicine and
plant medicine experiences. Robbie’s interest is in bridging the gap
between contemporary PAP approaches, which are strongly
academically (top down) influenced, with the learnings of therapists
and medicine people who have learnt directly from and with
Psychedelic medicines themselves.
